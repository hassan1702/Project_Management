## 🧭 Overview

This repository presents the group deliverables for the **Sydney Olympic Park Smart City Development**, completed as part of the **MGMT8040 Business Project Management** course at **Macquarie University**.

The project demonstrates my ability to lead and manage a real-world urban transformation initiative, applying:

* 🔧 Project implementation and monitoring
* 👥 Stakeholder coordination
* ♻️ Sustainability integration
* ⚡ Agile methodologies

> 🏅 *Sydney Olympic Park* was transformed from a 2000 Olympic venue into a **sustainable, tech-enabled urban precinct**, achieving a **6-Star Green Star rating**.

---

## 🚀 Project Highlights

### 🎯 **Objective**

Transform Sydney Olympic Park into a **smart city** by integrating **technology**, **sustainability**, and **community engagement**.

### 🏆 **Key Achievements**

* 🌱 Installed **smart infrastructure**: adaptive lighting, sensor-based water systems, smart waste.
* 🏅 Earned **6-Star Green Star** rating for sustainable development.
* 👥 Formed a **Smart City Advisory Panel** to involve the community.
* 💰 Managed **AUD 1.4 billion** budget (plus **AUD 2.7 billion** for Master Plan 2030).

### ⚠️ **Challenges Overcome**

* 🚚 Tackled **supply chain disruptions** and tech integration using agile techniques.
* 🌏 Balanced growth with **environmental preservation** (ESD principles).
* 🗣️ Overcame community resistance with proactive engagement and workshops.
